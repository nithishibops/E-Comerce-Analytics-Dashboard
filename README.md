# E-Commerce Analytics Dashboard

An interactive, end-to-end Power BI dashboard designed to track, analyze, and visualize critical e-commerce performance metrics. This project transforms raw e-commerce data into actionable business insights, helping stakeholders monitor sales trends, profitability, customer behaviors, and operational efficiency.

## 🚀 Key Features & Insights

*   **Key Performance Indicators (KPIs):** Real-time monitoring of **Total Sales ($40.00M)**, **Total Profit ($13.02M)**, **Total Orders (10K)**, and **Total Customers (2.89K)** with Year-over-Year (YoY) performance tracking.
*   **Trend Analysis:** Interactive monthly line charts comparing current **Sales & Profit Trends** directly against Previous Year (PY) benchmarks.
*   **Operational Tracking:** Split-second visibility into **Order Fulfillment Status** (Shipped, Pending, Delivered, Cancelled) and **Delivery Days Efficiency** (currently averaging 5.51 days).
*   **Customer & Product Breakdown:** Deep-dive into sales distribution by product category (Sports, Fashion, Books, etc.) and individual city performances (Kuwait City, Muscat, Riyadh, Dubai, and more).
*   **Payment Analysis:** Balanced distribution breakdown mapping consumer preferences across Wallets, Cards, Bank Transfers, and Cash on Delivery (COD).

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack & Skills Used

*   **Business Intelligence Tool:** Microsoft Power BI Desktop
*   **Data Modeling:** Star Schema architecture utilizing specialized Fact and Dimension tables (including a dedicated `Date Table`).
*   **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions):** Used for advanced Time Intelligence calculations (YoY growth %, PY Sales, PY Profit, and Profit Margins).
*   **UI/UX Design:** Dark-themed dashboard optimized for scannability, utilizing conditional formatting and clean KPI card layouts.
